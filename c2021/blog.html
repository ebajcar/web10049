    Midterm Information

    1. Written during regularly scheduled class in Week 7 Week 8 (first week after the break).. You will have 30 minutes to start the exam. You will have 90 minutes to complete it from the time you start.
    2. If you are currently in another time zone, you need to email me, to make arrangements to write it at a different time, no later than Friday, February 18, 2022.
    3. Covers all material from Weeks 1 through 6.
    4. Be prepared to demonstrate your mastery
      • of the learning outcomes 1, 2, 3, 6, 7, 8, 9, and 10 in the Course Outline.
      • by answering questions in form of multiple-choice (MC) , true-false (F/T), multiple-select (MS), fill-in-blanks (FIB), matching (MAT), ordering (ORD) and coding/written answers (WR).
      • approx. 30 MC two points each , approx. 20 F/T one point each, 50 total,
        10-12 MS, FIB, MAT, ORD, WR questions, 3-6 points each, 50 total.

    Preparing for Midterm

    1. Complete all homework (Worksheets 1-5).
    2. Study regularly and practice deliberately. Make connections to the Learning Outcomes.
    3. Make notes as you study and review your notes regularly.
    4. Join a Study Group, study together and test each other.
    5. Most questions are based on the material you are tasked to complete.
    6. Use Quiz 1, Quiz 2, and Quiz 3 to retest yourself.
    7. Participate on Piazza Q&A. I posted some practice questions. Ask questions, post answers, discuss.
