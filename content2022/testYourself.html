    Test Yourself!

    • 1 5 minutes Take a blank sheet of paper. Free-write everything you know or remember about the content of this module. Do not filter or evaluate what you are writing. Stop writing after five minutes.
    • 2 10 minutes Find a partner. With your partner, improve your answers. Continue writing on the same sheet. If you have an answer already, is it complete? is it correct? compare your answer with your partner. If you do not have an answer, write out one and compare with your partner. It is a good idea to write out the question with your answer. After fifteen minutes, stop the activity.
    • 3 15 minutesConsult your notes and resources. Identify what you have missed. Correct and complete your answers. Continue to work on completing and improving your answers outside of the class. You can share questions and answers on Piazza Q&A between classes.
    Repeat on a regular basis. Compare to previous performance. If you do not see significant progress, evaluate your study habits, find a study group or seek help such as tutoring.
