2.2. Lorem ipsum (dummy) text generators
Use instead of English. Generally users do not understand Latin so they focus on what you need them to validate your understanding of what they need. "It is a long established fact that a reader will be distracted by the readable content of a page when looking at its layout. The point of using Lorem Ipsum is that it has a more-or-less normal distribution of letters,... making it look like readable English."
Suggested generator: https://lipsum.com/. This site also provides images that can be used as placeholders, for the same reason.
